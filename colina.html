COLINA VADDO, GOA

Hidden away in the lush bylanes of Assagaon sits
Colina Villa, a Portuguese style village, in the heart of North Goa. Featuring nine individual, independent homes, this gated community in Goa offers privacy and exclusivity with a sense of belonging.

Consultant - Sameer Sawant
